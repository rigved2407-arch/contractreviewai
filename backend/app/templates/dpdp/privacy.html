Privacy Notice

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023

Compliance Status

Fully compliant with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act)

1. Data Fiduciary

Contract Review AI acts as the Data Fiduciary under the DPDP Act, 2023. We process personal data solely for the purpose of providing AI-powered contract review and legal analysis services to our clients.

2. Purpose of Processing

We process personal data exclusively for:

  • Contract document analysis and clause extraction
  • Risk assessment under Indian legal frameworks
  • Compliance reporting (DPDP Act, GST, TDS, etc.)
  • Service improvement and quality assurance

3. Lawful Basis

Processing is based on explicit consent obtained from Data Principals at the time of document upload, and/or contractual necessity under Section 4 of the DPDP Act, 2023.

4. Data Collected

We collect: name, email address, organizational affiliation, and document content uploaded for analysis. We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data. Any sensitive data within uploaded documents is processed incidental to the service.

5. Data Retention

Personal data is retained for 365 days from the date of collection. After this period, data is automatically deleted in compliance with Section 9 of the DPDP Act (Storage Limitation). Data may be deleted earlier upon request.

6. Data Principal Rights

Under Sections 11-14 of the DPDP Act, 2023, you have the right to:

Access Data

Request a copy of your data

Correct Data

Update inaccurate information

Erase Data

Request data deletion

Raise Grievance

File a complaint

7. Cross-Border Data Transfer

All personal data is stored and processed exclusively within India. No cross-border data transfer occurs, in full compliance with Section 16 of the DPDP Act, 2023. Data residency: {{ settings.data_residency or "India" }}.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable security safeguards including AES-256 encryption at rest, API key authentication, and comprehensive audit logging to prevent data breaches, as required under Section 8 of the DPDP Act.

9. Data Protection Officer

For DPDP-related queries, to exercise your rights, or to withdraw consent, contact:

Data Protection Officer

Email: dpo@contractreviewai.com

Response time: Within 30 days as required under Section 12 of the DPDP Act

10. Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach, we will notify the Data Protection Board of India and all affected Data Principals within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, as required under Section 8(6) of the DPDP Act.

11. Consent Withdrawal

You may withdraw your consent at any time by contacting the DPO or through the consent management dashboard. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing before such withdrawal.
